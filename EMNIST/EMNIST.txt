About this file: 

Training a model for classifying handwritten letters and digits in Extended-MNIST (EMNIST) dataset. 
The EMNIST include 52 letters (capital and small letters) and 10 digits. 
I used the default test dataset (emnist-byclass-test) to evaluate model.
